APUSH Timeline

1491 – Present

Unit 1: 1491–1607

Pre-Columbian societies, Columbian Exchange, Spanish colonization, Native American resistance.

Unit 2: 1607–1754

Development of British colonies, regional differences, transatlantic trade, beginnings of slavery.

Unit 3: 1754–1800

French & Indian War, American Revolution, Articles of Confederation, Constitution, Washington presidency.

Unit 4: 1800–1848

Jeffersonian democracy, Market Revolution, Jacksonian democracy, reform movements, Manifest Destiny.

Unit 5: 1844–1877

Sectionalism, Civil War, Reconstruction, abolition, 13th–15th Amendments.

Unit 6: 1865–1898

Industrialization, Gilded Age, labor movements, Populism, Native American displacement.

Unit 7: 1890–1945

Progressive reforms, WWI, Roaring Twenties, Great Depression, New Deal, WWII.

Unit 8: 1945–1980

Cold War, Civil Rights Movement, Vietnam War, Great Society, social change movements.

Unit 9: 1980–Present

Reagan Revolution, end of Cold War, globalization, 9/11, War on Terror, 21st-century politics.
